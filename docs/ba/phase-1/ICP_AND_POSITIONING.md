## Primary ICP

**Revit-heavy BIM teams** with real delivery pressure, especially:
- BIM Manager / Coordinator who needs review, governance, and pre-issue confidence
- BIMer / Drafter who needs fast understanding, query, and controlled action

## Secondary ICP

- Pro Developer / automation lead inside the same BIM organization
- Modular / MEP-heavy teams where review + controlled action + evidence matter more than generic AI chat

## Not primary for MVP

- early-stage generative design users
- pure scripting communities seeking maximum freedom over safety
- organizations expecting broad SaaS/cloud collaboration first

## Positioning statement

> 765T is an AI-ready BIM operating layer for production Revit teams: it helps teams understand projects faster, review quality with evidence, and execute low-risk actions through preview/approval guardrails.

## Competitive anchor

- vs pyRevit/Dynamo: easier, safer, less code-centric
- vs generic AI: has live Revit context and governed action
- vs Forma/Hypar/TestFit/Snaptrude: focuses on production BIM operations, not early design exploration
