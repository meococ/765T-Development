## Recruiting priority

1. BIM Manager / Coordinator from active delivery teams
2. BIMer / Drafter who uses Revit daily
3. Buyer / sponsor who can approve a pilot
4. Pro Developer / automation lead for extension/safety insight

## Sample quality rules

- Avoid recruiting only friendly internal champions.
- Include at least one skeptical or low-tooling participant.
- Avoid over-indexing on expert scripters if the product ICP is broader.
- If all sessions come from one company or one discipline, mark findings as limited.
